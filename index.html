Available mobile apps

Check out my Android apps on the Play Store!

Chestionare auto

Chestionare auto is an app I built to help people prepare for the Romanian driver's license exam. It features an exam simulator, a study mode, a legislation module and a statistics module where users can follow their progress.

Coduri poștale

Coduri poștale was created to assist people in finding the correct zip code for Romanian addresses. It provides users access to the entire Romanian zip codes catalog covering all counties and settlements in the country. It also features a reverse-search that allows users to find out which addresses are assigned to a specific zip code.

Romanian Companies

Romanian Companies was built to provide users access to the latest information on Romanian businesses. Users can search for companies based on their name or TIN, view their contact info, annual balances and more.

NBR Exchange Rates

NBR Exchange Rates gives users access to the latest foreign currency exchange rates published by the National Bank of Romania.

VAT Calculator

VAT Calculator provides the current VAT rates used in European Union countries. It also features a calculator that allows users to extract VAT from gross amounts, or calculate VAT for net amounts.
